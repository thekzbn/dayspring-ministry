About Dayspring Christian Ministry

Our journey of faith, purpose, and impact.

Our Founding Story

Dayspring Christian Ministry began in **2018** as a fellowship, eventually transitioning into a full ministry. It started with just **one dedicated member**, and by God's grace, we've seen daily additions to our family, growing stronger together.

Our Vision

WE ARE SAVED TO SAVE OTHERS

Our Mission

Luke 1:78-79: "Through the tender mercy of our God; whereby the dayspring from on high hath visited us, To give light to them that sit in darkness and in the shadow of death, to guide our feet into the way of peace."

Who We Serve

Our target audience includes **teenagers, youth, adults, students**, and everyone God brings our way. We believe in reaching out to all who seek guidance and community.
